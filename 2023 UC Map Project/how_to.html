Bearcat Positioning System

How To Use the Bearcat Positioning System (BPS):

Starting a route:

Step 1: Choose a building from the map on the screen. You select a building by clicking/tapping on it.

Step 2: Click the start navigation button.

Step 3: Follow the Route to your destination!

Ending a route:

Option 1:

Following the route to your final destination will end your route once you arrive.

Option 2:

If you decide to end your route prior to getting to your building, follow the steps below:

Step 1: Click the building you are currently routing to.

Step 2: Click the end route button.

Accessing Building Information:

Option 1:

Via Map/Building Location:

Step 1: Using the zoom feature on the map, locate the building you wish to access the information of.

Step 2: Click on the red icon to pull up building information. This information includes floor plans!

Option 2:

Via Building List:

Step 1: Navigate to the campus building list and identify the name of the desired building. Buildings are sorted based on primary purpose, divided into Libraries/Misc, Lecture Halls, and Residence Halls.

Step 2: Click on the name of the building to see the pop-up displayed on the map interface for that respective building

Option 3:

Via Search:

Step 1: Click on the search box located on the top right of the application.

Step 2: Enter the name of the building. A pop-up will appear above the building on the map with the available information displayed.
